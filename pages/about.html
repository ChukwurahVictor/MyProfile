About me

I have always been passionate about computers since I was a child and after my secondary school, I enrolled in a computer training school where I learnt the basics of computer repairs. My passion for computers also pushed me to apply to study Computer Engineering at the University of Benin where I am currently studying.

I started web development a little over a year ago having been introduced to it by a friend and I have since been learning and working hard to become a better developer. My goal is to one day become a world-class fullstack developer.

I see myself as a creative and inventive person who is always eager to learn, pays attention to detail and is passionate about meeting deadlines.
